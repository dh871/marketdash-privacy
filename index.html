3. Information We Process

3.1 Information you enter

  • Ticker symbols, watchlists, journal entries, and tool inputs.
  • Optional API keys you choose to paste (for example, Finnhub keys).

This information is stored locally in your app/webview storage (for example, local/session storage), so you can keep your settings between sessions.

3.2 Data received from external APIs

When you use live features, the App requests market/financial data from external providers. The request may include ticker symbols and your API token (if required by that provider).

3.3 Information we do not intentionally collect

  • We do not require name, phone number, or home address.
  • We do not create user accounts for this App.
  • We do not run ad personalization profiles.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide finance tool functionality inside the App.
  • To remember your local preferences and saved lists.
  • To fetch market and fundamentals data from provider APIs you enable.
  • To troubleshoot stability and improve app quality.

5. Data Storage and Retention

  • Most app data is stored locally on your device.
  • You can clear local data by deleting app data or uninstalling the App.
  • We do not maintain a centralized user account database for this App.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may connect to third-party services to retrieve finance data, including:

  • Finnhub
  • U.S. SEC EDGAR
  • Static asset CDNs used to load front-end libraries (for example, chart/file libraries).

These providers process requests under their own privacy policies and terms. We are not responsible for third-party policies.

7. Sharing and Selling Data

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not share your local app data with data brokers.
  • Data is only sent to providers needed to fulfill your request (for example, quote/news endpoints).

8. Security

We use reasonable safeguards for the app architecture, but no system is perfectly secure. Keep your device protected and do not share API keys you consider sensitive.

9. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Your Rights

Because most data is stored locally on your device, you generally control it directly (edit/delete/uninstall). For requests or questions, contact us at the email above.

11. International Use

If you access the App from outside your country, your data may be processed in locations where service providers operate.
